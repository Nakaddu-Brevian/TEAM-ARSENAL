About Arsenal

Pride of London Tradition. Passion. Excellence.

About Us

Founded in 1886, Arsenal FC has risen from humble beginnings in Woolwich to become one of the most celebrated clubs in world football. Known for its elegant style of play and commitment to excellence, Arsenal continues to inspire millions of fans across the globe.

Our mission is to blend football brilliance with community impact — developing young talent, promoting teamwork, and upholding the spirit of the beautiful game both on and off the pitch.

Our Legacy

From the historic days at Highbury to the modern glory of the Emirates Stadium, Arsenal’s journey is built on trophies, legendary players, and unwavering passion. The club’s philosophy of attractive, attacking football continues to define its identity — a symbol of pride, resilience, and unity.

Global Fanbase

With supporters spanning every continent, Arsenal stands as a global community united by love for the badge. Our fans’ passion drives the club forward season after season.

Leadership & Vision

Guided by Mikel Arteta, Arsenal’s modern philosophy blends youth, discipline, and tactical creativity shaping a new era of dominance and unity at the Emirates.

The Academy

The Arsenal Academy continues to produce exceptional talents nurturing young players who embody dedication, skill, and the Arsenal DNA.
