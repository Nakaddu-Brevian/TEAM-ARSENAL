Our Legacy

Founded in 1886, Arsenal Football Club has grown to become one of England’s most successful and storied football teams.
Renowned for its elegant attacking football, the club has won numerous domestic league titles, FA Cups, and other honors.
With a history of legendary players, iconic managers, and a global fanbase, Arsenal is celebrated not only for its achievements
on the pitch but also for its enduring culture, traditions, and commitment to entertaining football.”.

Match Highlights

Arsenal 3 - 1 Chelsea

A strong comeback victory at the Emirates Stadium as Arsenal overturned an early setback with grit, passion, and attacking brilliance. The Gunners grew stronger as the game progressed, dominating possession and creating wave after wave of pressure. Inspired performances from key players turned the tide, and a late surge secured a memorable win that thrilled the home crowd and reflected Arsenal’s trademark resilience and never-say-die attitude.

Liverpool 1 - 2 Arsenal

A thrilling away win with last-minute heroics as Arsenal delivered a masterclass in determination and teamwork. The Gunners fought back from behind, controlling possession and creating relentless pressure in the dying minutes. A dramatic late goal sealed the victory, sending the travelling fans into wild celebrations and reaffirming Arsenal’s reputation for courage, passion, and never giving up until the final whistle.

Arsenal 2 - 0 Manchester United

A dominant performance to secure top spot in the league as Arsenal showcased complete control from start to finish. The Gunners displayed fluid attacking football and solid defensive organization that left their opponents chasing shadows. Every player contributed with intensity and precision, turning the match into a statement of intent. The victory not only strengthened Arsenal’s position at the summit but also underlined their ambition and hunger to remain there.
