Club Overview & Heritage

The Arsenal Heritage

Celebrating over 130 years of passion, glory, and football excellence a legacy built on
teamwork, resilience, and the unwavering spirit that defines Arsenal Football Club.
From our humble beginnings in Woolwich to the grand Emirates Stadium, every era has been marked by
unforgettable victories, legendary players, and a deep connection with millions of fans around the world

Club Leadership & Achievements

  • Owner: Kroenke Sports & Entertainment
  • Manager: Mikel Arteta
  • Captain: Martin Ødegaard
  • Stadium: Emirates Stadium (Capacity: 60,704)
  • Major Honors: 13 League Titles, 14 FA Cups, 2 League Cups

Our Journey Through Time

Founded in 1886 in Woolwich, Arsenal began as a small workers’ club known as Dial Square. Over the decades, the Gunners have transformed into one of the world’s elite football institutions, known for their attacking football, loyal supporters, and strong values both on and off the pitch.

What Makes Arsenal Exceptional

Team Spirit

Arsenal’s success is rooted in unity and teamwork. Every player contributes to the vision of creating fast, creative, and attacking football admired worldwide.

Leadership

Under Mikel Arteta, Arsenal has entered a new era of confidence, tactical excellence, and youthful energy, blending experience with innovation for success.

Youth Development

The Arsenal Academy continues to nurture young talents who embrace discipline, skill, and the Gunners’ DNA, preparing them to shine at the highest levels.
