Arsenal 3 - 1 Chelsea

Arsenal staged a powerful comeback at the Emirates, dominating Chelsea in the second half. The Gunners showed tactical brilliance, scoring three impressive goals to secure a 3-1 victory.

Key performances came from the midfield duo and the forward line, who pressed high and maintained control throughout the match. This result strengthens Arsenal’s title hopes and boosts confidence heading into upcoming fixtures.

Arsenal’s medical team has issued an update on key players ahead of the next Premier League fixture. The team confirmed that several stars are in the final stages of recovery and expected to return soon.

The manager emphasized squad depth and rotation to maintain performance levels as Arsenal prepares for a crucial run of fixtures across all competitions.
